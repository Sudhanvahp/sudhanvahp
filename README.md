## 🚀 About Me

<img align="right" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/212748842-9fcbad5b-6173-4175-8a61-521f3dbb7514.gif" width="340" alt="coding animation" />

I'm an **Application Developer** specializing in **Computer Vision** and **Vision AI**, focused on
turning deep-learning models into real, deployable products. My core interest is **perception for
robotics** — giving machines the ability to see, understand, and act on the world around them.

- 🔭 **Currently building:** Vision AI for robots — real-time detection, tracking & inference.
- 🧠 **Deep into:** object detection, image segmentation, and edge deployment.
- 🛠️ **I bridge the gap:** from research notebooks → production-grade applications.
- 🌱 **Always learning:** new model architectures, optimization, and MLOps.
- 🎯 **Goal:** ship vision systems that are fast, accurate, and reliable in the real world.
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I believe the best model is the one that actually runs on the robot.
